Core Features

Everything your classroom needs

One platform combining attendance, timetable & student productivity — built for the modern campus.

QR Code Attendance

Dynamic QR codes generated every class, expiring within minutes to prevent proxy attendance.

Face Recognition

Computer-vision powered face detection that marks attendance instantly when students walk in.

Fingerprint Biometric

Tamper-proof fingerprint scanner integration for the most secure attendance verification.

Productivity Hub

During free periods, get personalized suggestions — coding tasks, aptitude tests, MOOCs & career skills.

Smart Timetable

Auto-synced timetable with real-time updates, free-slot detection & rescheduling alerts.

Faculty Notifications

Faculty can mark unavailability; free faculty can claim slots — students get instant push alerts.

06
