// Core Features

Everything your classroom needs

A single platform combining attendance, scheduling, and student productivity — engineered for speed and simplicity.

QR Code Attendance

Dynamic QR codes refresh every class and expire in minutes — eliminating proxy attendance entirely.

Face Recognition

Computer-vision powered detection marks attendance the moment students walk into the room.

Fingerprint Biometric

Tamper-proof biometric scanner integration — the most secure way to verify physical presence.

Productivity Hub

Personalized free-period activities — coding challenges, aptitude tests, MOOCs and career prep.

Smart Timetable

Auto-synced schedules with real-time updates, free-slot detection and rescheduling alerts.

Faculty Notifications

Mark unavailability, claim open slots, push instant alerts — communication built into the workflow.

// Methodology

From idea to deployment in 6 steps

A disciplined engineering process — every feature designed, validated, and refined for the real classroom.

01 Discovery

Understand the problem

Identify pain points in manual attendance and how free periods get wasted in real classrooms.

02 Research

Collect & model data

Aggregate timetables, student records, and attendance history into a clean unified schema.

03 Build

Attendance pipelines

Implement QR, face-recognition and fingerprint flows with offline fallback and audit trails.

04 Personalise

Recommend activities

Suggest coding, aptitude, MOOCs and career prep matched to each student's goals and gaps.

05 Integrate

Unify the experience

Combine attendance, timetable, productivity and analytics in a single coherent dashboard.

06 Ship

Test, validate, deploy

Validate workflows with faculty & students, iterate quickly, then roll out across departments.
